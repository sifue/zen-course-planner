## 概要

 [FROM CLASS FIELD THEORY OF ANABELIAN GEOMETRY AND IUT]
This most advanced part of IUT courses, IUT4 will present areas of modern number theory related and used in the IUT theory, they include some of class field theory, anabelian geometry, and IUT.       

(日本語)
[遠アーベル幾何とIUTの類対論]
IUTコースの最も進んだ部分であるIUT4（本授業）では、IUT理論に関連し、使用されている現代の数論の分野を紹介する。それらには、類体論、遠アーベル幾何学、およびIUTが含まれている。
（なお、この授業は英語で開講する。）

## 到達目標

IUT 4 uses all of the previous parts IUT1-IUT3 and goes much further, towards anabelian geometry and IUT. The theories will be presented at the level of some of their main ideas, concepts and objects, to enable further self-study of the IUT theory. 

(日本語)
IUT4（本授業）では、IUT1-IUT3で学修した内容を全て用い、遠アーベル幾何学とIUTに向けた学修を進める。
本授業で学修する理論は、主要となる考え、概念、対象のレベルで紹介され、IUT理論の更なる自己学修が可能となる。

## 講義計画

| 回 | タイトル | 内容 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Guidance class: from number theory of the 20th century to anabelian geometry and to IUT | Introductory lecture to inform the students about what will follow |
| 2 | Projective limits of groups and rings | An importan algebraic concept which is a bridge from finite to infinite |
| 3 | On class field theory 1 | Informal discussions and presentations of the core part of modern number theory: class field theory |
| 4 | On class field theory 2 | Continued |
| 5 | The value of adelic methods | A presentation of adelic objects and discussions of their properties |
| 6 | On three main generalisations of class field theory | General discussion of the key directions of modern number theory as stemming from class field theory |
| 7 | Towards anabelian geometry | Introduction into basic notions of anabelian geometry |
| 8 | Hyperbolic curves over number fields | Fusion of geometry and algebra at the example of hyperbolic curves over number fields |
| 9 | Absolute mono-anabelian geometry of Mochizuki | General discussion and presentation of some of key novel features of anabelian geometry |
| 10 | More on absolute mono-anabelian geometry | continued |
| 11 | Etale-theta function theory of Mochizuki | General discussion of aspects of this theory |
| 12 | Etale-theta function theory and IUT-2 | continued |
| 13 | Theatres as a generalisation of adeles, to take into account both geometry and arithmetic | General discussion of theatres in IUT, as a far reaching generalisation of adeles |
| 14 | Log-theta lattice in IUT | General discussion of two non-ring links in IUT as a way to go far beyond conventional number theory |
| 15 | Concluding class: applying IUT to abc inequalities about integers | General discussion of how applications of IUT reveal most fundamental properties of integer numbers (IUT-1) |
