## 到達目標

The goals of IUT1 are to provide a basic introduction to modern number theory and to teach some algebraic techniques used in number theory.
By the end of the module, students should be able to understand a range of concepts in number theory, have a knowledge of principal theorems and their proofs as treated, and appreciate the importance of number theory.

(日本語)
IUT1（本授業）では、モダンな数論への導入と、数論で使用されるいくつかの代数的技術を教える。
本授業を修了した学生は、数論の様々な概念を理解し、主要な定理とそれらが扱われる証明についての知識を修得し、数論の重要性を理解することができる。

## 講義計画

| 回 | タイトル | 内容 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Guidance class: integer numbers | first meeting to slowly introduce the ring of integers |
| 2 | The ring Z of integer numbers | second meeting to slowly introduce the ring of integers |
| 3 | Ideals in Z as its natural algebraic subobjects | moving to a higher perspective to talk about ideals |
| 4 | Division algorithm | division algorithms as a core |
| 5 | Maximal and prime ideals of Z | description of those and their properties |
| 6 | More general rings and ideals | going from Z to more general rings, as a way of abstraction which brings many fruits |
| 7 | Building new rings | ways to construct new rings, in order to see deeper |
| 8 | The main theorem on ring homomorphisms | relations between rings |
| 9 | Quotient rings of Z and related theorems | the crucial operation of quotinet rings explained slowly |
| 10 | Linear and quadratic congruences | historical congruences as properties of rings |
| 11 | More on quadratic aspects | non-linear aspects as prevailing in modern math |
| 12 | p-adic numbers 1 | the fundamentally important p-adic numbers invented more than 100 years ago and playing a key role in math |
| 13 | p-adic numbers 2 | continued |
| 14 | The new world of p-adic numbers | continued |
| 15 | Concluding class: the need to use algebra for number theoretical studies | numerous examples and illustrations of the importance to go beyond the appearance into deeper issues |
