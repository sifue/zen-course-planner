## 概要

 [ALGEBRA AND GEOMETRY FOR NUMBER THEORY]
IUT 2 provides more information on basic number theory, commutative algebra, geometry and elliptic curves.

本授業では、基本的な、数論、可換代数、幾何学、および楕円曲線の概念に関して、IUT1よりもさらに詳しい内容を学ぶ。
（なお、この授業は英語で開講する。）

## 到達目標

IUT2 will present to the students some key notions of commutative algebra and algebraic geometry such as curves. By the end of the module the students should 
be familiar with and understand a range of concepts of the subject; be able to understand important results from the theories; be able to solve unseen problems using the knowledge and skills acquired. 

(日本語)
IUT2（本授業）では、学生に可換代数と代数幾何においていくつかの鍵となる概念、例えば曲線などを提供する。
本授業を修得した学生は、主題の様々な概念を理解し、馴染み、理論からの重要な結果を理解し、獲得した知識とスキルを使用して未知の問題を解決することができる。

## 講義計画

| 回 | タイトル | 内容 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Guidance class: adding more abstract algebra and some geometry for the use in number theory | explaining the need of abstact algebra and geometry for number theory, to make students appreciate the abstract study |
| 2 | More algebra: modules over rings | modules as a slight generalisation of vector spaces |
| 3 | Finiteness condition: noetherian modules | finiteness conditions in algebra |
| 4 | The use of noetherian property | applications of finiteness condition |
| 5 | Finitely generated modules over PID | the main theorem about fin generated modules over PID |
| 6 | Prime ideals of a commutative ring | leading to the notion of spectrum of a comm ring |
| 7 | Localisation | studying things locally |
| 8 | Curves over fields 1 | geometry of curves |
| 9 | Curves over fields 2 | geometry of curves |
| 10 | Divisors of curves as an analogue of ideals | algebraic objects useful for the study of geometry of curves |
| 11 | Differentials | fusion of algebra and analysis to study curves |
| 12 | Linear systems associated to divisors | from algebra to geometry |
| 13 | Elliptic curves 1 | the frontier of the curves theory |
| 14 | Elliptic curves 2 | continuation |
| 15 | Concluding class: using geometry for number theoretical studies | summarising the value of geometrical vision for number theory, to make students appreciate the importance of using geometry for the study of numbers |
